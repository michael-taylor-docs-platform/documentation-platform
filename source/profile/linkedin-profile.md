# Michael Taylor — Professional Profile

## Headline

Enterprise Knowledge Management & Documentation Platform Leader | CI/CD, Governance, AI-Ready Content Systems | Cybersecurity

---

## About

Michael Taylor leads enterprise knowledge and documentation platforms as **business-critical infrastructure**, not content production functions.

With over 15 years of experience building and governing documentation ecosystems within complex technology environments, he operates at the intersection of:

- Knowledge strategy  
- Platform architecture  
- Release governance  
- Engineering execution  

His work focuses on transforming documentation from static content into **measurable, scalable systems** that directly support:

- Product velocity  
- Customer trust  
- Developer experience  

---

## Leadership Approach

Michael applies a **systems-driven approach** to knowledge management, treating documentation as an integrated component of the software delivery lifecycle.

He emphasizes:

- Structured, validated content systems over ad hoc documentation
- Governance frameworks that scale across product portfolios
- Tight integration with engineering workflows and CI/CD pipelines
- Measurable outcomes tied to release quality and operational reliability

He is known for aligning documentation strategy with engineering and product priorities, ensuring that knowledge systems evolve alongside platform architecture.

## Key Contributions Across Career

- Built and led release-critical documentation pipelines integrated with engineering CI/CD workflows

- Partnered closely with engineering, product, and cybersecurity teams to align documentation with delivery cycles  
- Positioned documentation as operational infrastructure rather than support output

## Perspective on AI and Knowledge Systems

Michael views AI as a **capability multiplier** within modern knowledge ecosystems.

His approach prioritizes:

- Structured and validated content as the foundation for AI systems  
- Metadata-driven architectures enabling reliable retrieval and reasoning  
- Safety-first integration aligned with governance and quality controls  
- Practical implementation over hype-driven adoption  

This perspective aligns closely with retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) and AI-assisted documentation systems, where content quality and structure directly impact system performance.
